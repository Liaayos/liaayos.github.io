Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to bring everyone a great opportunity to enjoy nature and be part of it, with Scoots you will be getting direct touch with nature and knowing how to feel nature in its more open side. We want you to take with you a unique experience and to come back for more, we want to overcome your fears and be free, and we want to be part of that important moment in your life, with Scoots you not only getting a service, you will be finding yourself and a open door to nature.

Audience

This page is focused for an audience of people who wants to explore more and have new experiences, anyone can join us and experience this contact with nature, you can be part of a exploration team, or try to live your life beyond your limits, or maybe you just want to get out of the routine, to get out of your office and try new things, maybe you are a young person and want to start your journey with nature and adventure, if you want to be out of your comfort zone, you want to keep going on your adventures or just want to star your journey with nature and adventure, come with us and overcome your fears and routine. This audience will access the site by desktop and laptop.
