Audience

This website is created for people that love to know about the weather and enjoys the experience, people who wants to know more about others places' weather, people that needs weather in daily basis, or for traveling.

Scenarios

1) Paola is going for a flight today and wants to know the weather in the destination. 2) Juan David wants to take a break from working and wants to go out with his wife. 3) Paola wants to plan a trip for vacations in the beach and needs to know the wether in June. 4) Juan David is having his friend's wedding and wants to know the wether, to be sure is not going to rain. 5) Paola is having her son's birthday and wants to celebrate in the park, she would like to know the weather.
